Our Services

We are pleased to offer the following services to our esteemed patients:

  • Disease name

    Our doctor gives the name of the disease after diagnosis of the symptoms the patient has keyed into the system.

  • Disease Description

    The doctor offers to give more information about the disease so the patient can understand what they are suffering from.

  • Medicine Recommendation

    The doctor then gives prescribtion of the medicine for the patient to take for them to get better.

  • Suggest Precautions

    The patient is given precautions to take inorder to prevent further damages by the disease they are suffering.

  • Diet Recommendations

    Healthy meal diet suggestion is given to the patient to strenghthen their immunity and improve their recovery.

  • Exercise To Do:

    Physical activities are recommended for the physical health of the patient and keep them in strong in good condition.
